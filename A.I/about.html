HAMPOS was established in the year 2000 with the aim of providing quality education to students from diverse backgrounds. Over the years, the school has grown significantly and has become one of the most sought-after educational institutions in Kaduna.

HAMPOS history is rooted in the belief that every student has the potential to succeed and it is our mission to help them realize that potential. Our founding principles were to create a learning environment that was inclusive, nurturing, and inspiring, and this is something that we continue to strive for today.

HAMPOS mission is to provide a high-quality education that is accessible to all students, regardless of their background or circumstances. We believe that every student has the right to receive an education that will equip them with the skills and knowledge they need to succeed in the world. Our goal is to create a learning environment that is supportive, challenging, and inspiring, and to help students develop the confidence and independence they need to succeed in life.

HAMPOS values are deeply rooted in the belief that education is the key to success, and that every student has the potential to make a positive impact in the world. Our values include respect, responsibility, honesty, and a commitment to excellence. These values are reflected in everything we do, from the way we teach and learn, to the way we interact with each other and the community.

At HAMPOS, we understand the importance of providing students with a well-rounded education. That is why we offer a range of programs, including academic, athletic, and extracurricular activities. Our academic programs are designed to meet the needs of students at every stage of their education, from early childhood through high school. Our athletic programs provide opportunities for students to engage in physical activity and develop teamwork skills, while our extracurricular activities help students explore their interests and develop new skills.

One of the hallmarks of our school is our commitment to creating a safe and inclusive learning environment. We believe that all students should feel valued and respected, and that everyone should have the opportunity to succeed. That is why we have a zero-tolerance policy for bullying and discrimination, and why we offer a range of support services to help students who are struggling.

In conclusion, our HAMPOS has a rich history and a clear mission and values. We are dedicated to providing quality education to students from diverse backgrounds and helping them develop the skills and knowledge they need to succeed in the world. We believe that every student has the potential to make a positive impact, and we are committed to helping them achieve their goals and realize their dreams.
