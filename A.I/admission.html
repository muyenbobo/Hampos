Admission to Hampos College is a competitive process and requires a completed application, including all required documents.
To apply, prospective students must: Submit an online application form, which can be found on the college's website.

Provide official transcripts from all previous high schools or colleges attended.

Submit ACT or SAT scores.

Write a personal statement essay explaining why you want to attend Hampos College and what makes you a good fit for the college.

Submit two letters of recommendation from teachers or community leaders who can attest to your academic abilities and character.

Pay a non-refundable application fee of $150.

All applicants are encouraged to apply by the early decision deadline, which is December 1st. Applications received after December 1st will be considered on a rolling basis until the class is full.

Admission decisions will be communicated to applicants by email and mail. Accepted students will be required to submit a deposit to secure their place in the incoming class.

We encourage all applicants to visit the campus and attend one of our open houses to learn more about Hampos College. If you have any questions about the application process, please feel free to contact the admissions office.
